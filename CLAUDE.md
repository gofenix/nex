## Commit Message Convention
Follow [Conventional Commits](https://www.conventionalcommits.org/):
- **Format**: `<type>(<scope>): <subject>`
- **Strict Rule**: **NO triple backticks (```)** in the message.
- Subject: ≤ 50 chars, imperative mood.
