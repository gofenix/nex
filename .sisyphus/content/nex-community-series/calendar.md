- One Chinese-first draft package per week.
- English adaptation only starts after the Chinese version is approved.
- Primary channel means the first English community destination after approval.
- Backup channel is the second destination only if the post lands cleanly and fits community rules.

## Sequencing Notes
- Weeks 1-2 establish motive and workflow philosophy.
- Weeks 3-4 prove the philosophy with small, inspectable examples.
- Weeks 5-6 sharpen credibility with boundaries and shipped momentum.
- Weeks 7-8 broaden into showcase and cross-community case-study material.
- No adjacent weeks share both the same primary audience and the same proof shape.
